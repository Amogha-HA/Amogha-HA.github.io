The Institute Chess culture
These are my personal views

The Dark knight chess club(DKCC) of IIT Bombay is one of the most active clubs that is present in the institute. It organizes around 20 tournaments in a year for players of all categories like UG,PG and for professors.

We also have an institute rating system.Unlike the "K" factor(factor of change) used by the FIDE, here we use a normal system defined by Swiss Perfect. For those who donot know what is Swiss Perfect, it is a software used for automatic pairing of players in the tournament.

Coming to the tournament part, every undergraduate student gets to start his Chess career in the institute, by playing the Institute Chess open. Then comes the Freshie Chess workshop, for the freshmen batch of the respective year, followed by a Freshie Chess weekend.

It is followed by many more events, the next main event being The Inter IIT Selection. Inter IIT is the most prestigious tournament we IITians get to play.These are the events which occur in the autumn semester.

The spring semester is meant to decide the Official Chess champion of IIT Bombay! The Candidates tournament will be held somewhere around mid March. The winner of the candidates tournament gets to challenge the previous Institute champion.

Chess here in the institute is not about becoming a champion.It is about how well you understand the game of chess and implement it in your life.We get to meet new people, learn new things and organize events on our own.

If you are still not into chess in the institute, you are missing one of the best things, I must say.
Give it a try.You will enjoy it!
